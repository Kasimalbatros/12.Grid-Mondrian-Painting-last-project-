Mondrian Painting(project).

Concept how to make mondrain painting.
1.Make whole background-color black and give gap property so that in between boxes black gap visible.
2.Container should contains Height and Width of box
Inside Items below commands are used.
3.grid-column:span 3;  (merge or takes space of 3 columns into single column)
4.grid-row:span 2;    (merge or takes space of 2 rows into single column)
5.grid-area:2/2/4/4   (Please practice grid area)
